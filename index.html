Orange Med Planner

During the From Scratch Hackathon, our team designed Orange Med Planner, an innovative application made in no-code with "Adalo". The application allows Orange employees to plan and manage their medical appointments with ease. Among its key features: A user-friendly interface to make appointments in a few clicks, An integrated calendar for a better visualization of availability and Automatic notifications to remind you of upcoming consultations. Thanks to its intuitive design and usefulness, Orange Med Planner won first place in the competition.
